
\begin{slide}{EXAFS: $\chi(k)$ and XAFS Fourier Transforms}

XAFS is an {\RedEmph{interference effect}}, using the wave-nature of the
photo-electron.  We express the XAFS in terms of {\RedEmph{photo-electron
    wavenumber}}, ${k}$:

\[  k= \sqrt{  \frac{2m(E-E_0)}{ {\hbar}^2} } \]

We'll also then use Fourier Transforms to convert from $k$ to  $R$.
